
1  海外东经:
海外自东南陬至东北陬者。
The overseas regions from the southeastern corner to the northeastern corner.
2  海外东经:
𨲠丘，爰有遗玉、青马、视肉、杨柳、甘柤、甘华，甘果所生。在东海，两山夹丘，上有树木。一曰嗟丘，一曰百果所在，在尧葬东。
Leiqiu, where there are left-behind jade, green horses, Shirou, willows, sweet zha, sweet hua, and sweet fruits that grow. In the East Sea, two mountains enclose a hill, with trees growing on it. One is called Jieqiu, and one is where all kinds of fruits grow; they are located east of Yao's burial site.
3  海外东经:
大人国在其北，为人大，坐而削舡。一曰在𨲠丘北。
The Guo of the Great People lies to its north. The people there are very tall, and they sit while carving boats. One account says it is located north of Leiqiu.
4  海外东经:
奢比之尸在其北，兽身、人面、大耳，珥两青蛇。一曰肝榆之尸在大人北。
The corpse of Shebi lies to its north, with a beast's body and a human face, large ears, and two green snakes dangling from them. One account says the corpse of Ganyu is located north of the Guo of the Great People.
5  海外东经:
君子国在其北，衣冠带剑，食兽，使二大虎在旁，其人好让不争。有薰华草，朝生夕死。一曰在肝榆之尸北。
The Guo of the Gentlemen lies to its north; they wear caps and robes, carry swords at their waists, eat beasts, and have two large tigers by their sides. The people there like to yield and do not quarrel. There is a plant called Xunhuacaowhich grows in the morning and dies at night. One account says it lies to the north of the corpse of Ganyu.
6  海外东经:
𧈫𧈫在其北，各有两首。一曰在君子国北。
The Xianxians lie to its north, each having two heads. One account says they are located north of the Guo of Gentlemen.
7  海外东经:
朝阳之谷，神曰天吴，是为水伯、在𧈫𧈫北两水间。其为兽也，八首人面，八足八尾，皆青黄。
The Chongyang Valley has a deity named Tianwu, who is the god of water; he resides between two rivers to the north of Xianxians. As a beast, it has eight heads with human faces, eight legs, and eight tails; all are blue-yellow in color.
8  海外东经:
青丘国在其北，其狐四足九尾。一曰在朝阳北。
The Guo of Qingqiu lies to its north, where the foxes have four feet and nine tails. One account says it is located to the north of Chongyang.
9  海外东经:
帝命竖亥步，自东极至于西极，五亿十选九千八百步。竖亥右手把算，左手指青丘北。一曰禹令竖亥。一曰五亿十万九千八百步。
The emperor ordered Shuhai to measure, from the easternmost point to the westernmost point, a distance of five hundred million ten qian nine thousand eight hundred steps. Shuhai held an abacus in his right hand and pointed with his left hand toward the north of Qingqiu. One account says that Yu ordered Shuhai. One account says five hundred million ten thousand nine thousand eight hundred steps.
10  海外东经:
黑齿国在其北，为人黑，食稻啖蛇，一赤一青在其旁。一曰在竖亥北，为人黑手，食稻使蛇，其一蛇赤。
The Guo of Heichi lies to its north; the people there are black-skinned, eat rice and snakes, with one red snake and one green snake by their side. One account says it is to the north of Shuhai, where the people have black hands, eat rice, and use snakes; one of the snakes is red.
11  海外东经:
下有汤谷。汤谷上有扶桑，十日所浴，在黑齿北。居水中，有大木，九日居下枝，一日居上枝。
ල Beneath it lies the Hot Spring Valley. Above the Hot Spring Valley is Fusang, where ten suns bathe; it is located to the north of Heichi. It resides in water, with a large tree present: nine suns dwell on its lower branches and one sun dwells on its upper branch.
12  海外东经:
雨师妾在其北，其为人黑，两手各操一蛇，左耳有青蛇，右耳有赤蛇。一曰在十日北，为人黑身人面，各操一龟。
Yushiqie lies to its north; this being is black in color, holding a snake in each hand, with a green snake in the left ear and a red snake in the right ear. One account says it is located to north of the ten suns, where this being has a black body and human face, each holding a turtle.
13  海外东经:
玄股之国在其北，其为人衣鱼食䳼，使两鸟夹之。一曰在雨师妾北。
The Guo of Xuangu lies to its north; the people wear fish as clothing and eat birds, with two birds flanking them. One account says it is located in the north of Yushiqie.
14  海外东经:
毛民之国在其北，为人身生毛。一曰在玄股北。
The Guo of Maomin lies to its north; these people have hair growing on their bodies. One account says it is located in northern Xuangu.
15  海外东经:
劳民国在其北，其为人黑。或曰教民。一曰在毛民北，为人面目手足尽黑。
The Guo of Laomin lies to its north, and the people there are black. Some say it is Jiaomin. One account says it lies north of Maomin, where the people's faces, hands, and feet are all black.
16  海外东经:
东方勾芒，鸟身人面，乘两龙。
In the east is Goumang; he has a bird body and human face, riding two dragons.
17  海外东经:
建平元年四月丙戌，待诏太常属臣望校治，侍中光禄勋臣龚、侍中奉车都尉光禄大夫臣秀领主省。
In the first year of Jianping, on the Bingxu day of the fourth month (April 28, 397 AD), the official Daizhao Taichang Shuchen Wang examined and corrected it. Attending Minister Guangluxun Chengu, Attending Minister Fengche Duyue Guangludafu Chenshow oversaw its review.
