24 打开字典	大荒东经:	
有困民国，勾姓而食。有人曰王亥，两手操鸟，方食其头。王亥托于有易，河伯仆牛。有易杀王亥，取仆牛。河念有易，有易潜出，为国于兽，方食之，名曰摇民。帝舜生戏，戏生摇民。
There is a country called Kunmin, whose people have the surname Gou and eat food. There was a man named Wanghai, who held birds in both hands while eating their heads. Wanghai entrusted himself to Youyi; Hebo's oxen were servants. Youyi killed Wanghai and seized the servant oxen. Hebo missed Youyi, so Youyi secretly left and founded a country among beasts, where they ate them; this people was called Yaomin. Emperor Shun gave birth to Xi, who in turn begot the Yaomin people.

Therefore, there were frequent droughts; only when the people imitated Yinglong's form during a drought would heavy rain finally come.
35 打开字典显示相似段落	大荒东经:	
东海中有流波山，入海七千里。其上有兽，状如牛，苍身而无角，一足，出入水则必风雨，其光如日月，其声如雷，其名曰夔。黄帝得之，以其皮为鼓，橛以雷兽之骨，声闻五百里，以威天下。
In the East Sea there is Liubo Shan, which extends into the sea for seven thousand li. There is a beast on it, resembling an ox with a blue body and no horns, having only one foot. When it enters or leaves the water there must be wind and rain; its light resembles that of the sun and moon; its sound is like thunder. Its name is Kui. Huangdi obtained it, and used its hide to make a drum, fastening it with the bones of Leishou. The sound could be heard for five hundred li, using it to awe the world.
