东北海之外，大荒之中，河水之间，附禺之山，帝颛顼与九嫔葬焉。爰有𩿨久、文贝、离俞、鸾鸟、凰鸟、大物、小物。有青鸟、琅鸟、玄鸟、黄鸟、虎、豹、熊、罴、黄蛇、视肉、璇、瑰、瑶、碧，皆出卫于山。丘方圆三百里，丘南帝俊竹林在焉，大可为舟。竹南有赤泽水，名曰封渊。有三桑无枝。丘西有沉渊，颛顼所浴。
Beyond the East and North Seas, in the midst of the great wilderness between the Yellow River and the water, there is Fu Yu Mountain. Emperor Zhuanxu and his nine consorts were buried there. There are also Huijiu, Wenbei, Liyu, luan bird, huang bird, great things, and small things. There are green birds, Langniao, Xuan Niao, yellow birds, tigers, leopards, bears, pines, yellow snakes, Shirou, Xuan, Gui, Yao, and Bi. All of these originate from Weiyu Mountain. The hill is round with a diameter of three hundred li. To the south of the hill, there is a bamboo forest belonging to Emperor Jun; the bamboo is so large that it can be used to make boats. To the south of the bamboo grove flows a red-colored water, named Fengyuan. There are three mulberry trees without branches. To the west of the hill is a deep abyss, where Emperor Zhuanxu bathed.

15 打开字典显示相似段落	大荒北经:

In the midst of the great waste land, there is a mountain named Chengdu Zaitian. There was a man wearing two yellow snakes in his ears and holding two other yellow snakes; his name was Kuafu. Houtu gave birth to Xin, who in turn gave birth to Kuafu. Kuafu overestimated his strength and wanted to chase the sun's shadow; he caught up with it at Yugou. He drank from the Yellow River but was still not quenched, and intended to run toward a great marsh; before he arrived there, he died at this place. Yinglong had already killed Chiyou and then also killed Kuafu; thus, he went to the south and stayed there. Hence, the southern regions have much rain.

17 打开字典显示相似段落	大荒北经:	
共工臣名曰相繇，九首蛇身，自环，食于九土。其所歍所尼，即为源泽，不辛乃苦，百兽莫能处。禹湮洪水，杀相繇，其血腥臭，不可生谷，其地多水，不可居也。禹湮之，三仞三沮，乃以为池，群帝是因以为台。在昆仑之北。
A minister of Gonggong named Xiangyao had nine heads and a snake-like body; he encircled himself, feeding on the soil from nine regions. Wherever he stepped or lingered became a marsh; its water was not spicy but bitter, and no beasts could dwell there. Yu controlled the floodwaters and killed Xiangyao; his blood was foul-smelling, making it impossible to grow grain there. The land had much water and could not be inhabited. Yu tried to block the waters three times, but each time it failed; finally, he made it into a pond, and all emperors used this place as a terrace. It was located north of Kunlun Mountain.

20 打开字典显示相似段落	大荒北经:	
有系昆之山者，有共工之台，射者不敢北向。有人衣青衣，名曰黄帝女魃。蚩尤作兵伐黄帝，黄帝乃令应龙攻之冀州之野。应龙畜水，蚩尤请风伯、雨师，纵大风雨。黄帝乃下天女曰魃。雨止，遂杀蚩尤。魃不得复上，所居不雨。叔均言之帝，后置之赤水之北，叔均乃为田祖。魃时亡之。所欲逐之者，令曰：“神北行！”先除水道，决通沟渎。
There is a mountain called Xikun; on it stands the terrace of Gonggong, and archers dare not aim northward toward it. There was a person dressed in green clothes, whose name was Huangdi Nüba. Chiyou made weapons to attack the Yellow Emperor; thus, the Yellow Emperor ordered Yinglong to attack him in the plains of Jizhou. Yinglong gathered water, and Chiyou summoned Fengbo and Yushi to unleash a great wind and rain. The Yellow Emperor then sent down from heaven a female deity named Ba. When the rain stopped, they proceeded to kill Chiyou. Ba could not return to heaven and wherever she dwelled, there was no rain. Shujun reported this to the emperor; later, he placed her north of Chishui River, and Shujun then became the ancestor of farming. Ba would sometimes disappear. Those who wished to chase her were ordered: "The deity is going north!" First, clear the waterways and dredge the ditches.

32 打开字典显示相似段落	大荒北经:	
西北海之外，赤水之北，有章尾山。有神，人面蛇身而赤，直目正乘，其瞑乃晦，其视乃明，不食，不寝，不息，风雨是谒。是烛九阴，是谓烛龙。
Beyond the northwest sea, north of Chishui River, there is Zhangwei Mountain. There was a deity with human face, red snake body, straight eyes and upright posture. When it closed its eyes, darkness fell; when it opened them, light appeared. It did not eat, sleep, or breathe, but summoned winds and rains. It illuminated the nine shades of darkness; this is called Zhulong.
