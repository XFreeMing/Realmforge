1  海外北经:
海外自东北陬至西北陬者。
From the northeasternmost corner to the northwesternmost corner of the overseas regions.
2  海外北经:
无𦜹之国在长股东，为人无𦜹。
The state of Wuju is located east of Changgu, and its people have no eyebrows.
3  海外北经:
锺山之神，名曰烛阴，视为昼，暝为夜，吹为冬，呼为夏，不饮，不食，不息，息为风，身长千里。在无𦜹之东。其为物，人面蛇身，赤色，居锺山下。
The deity of Zhongshan was named Zhu Yin. When he opened his eyes, it became daylight; when he closed them, it turned to night. His breath brought winter, and his exhalation brought summer. He did not drink, eat, or breathe; yet when he did breathe, it became wind. His body was a thousand li long. It lies to the east of Wuju. Its form was that of a human face and a serpent's body, red in color, residing beneath Zhongshan Mountain.
4  海外北经:
目国在其东，一目中其面而居。一曰有手足。
The state of Muguo lies to its east; the people there have one eye located at the center of their face and live accordingly. One account says they also have hands and feet.
5  海外北经:
柔利国在一目东，为人一手一足，反膝，曲足居上。一云留利之国，人足反折。
The state of Rouliguo lies to the east of Muguo; its people have one hand and one foot, their knees are reversed, and their bent feet are placed above. One account says it is the state of Liuli, where the people's legs are bent backward.
6  海外北经:
共工之臣曰相柳氏，九首，以食于九山。相柳之所抵，厥为泽溪。禹杀相柳，其血腥，不可以树五谷种。禹厥之，三仞三沮，乃以为众帝之台。在昆仑之北，柔利之东。相柳者，九首人面，蛇身而青。不敢北射，畏共工之台。台在其东，台四方，隅有一蛇，虎色，首冲南方。
A minister of Gonggong named Xiangliu Shi had nine heads and ate from nine mountains. Where Xiangliu struck, it became marshy valleys. Yu killed Xiangliu; its blood was foul and could not be used to plant the five grains. Yu dug there, but three times he tried and three times it flooded; eventually, he made it into a terrace for many deities. It lies north of Kunlun and east of Rouli. Xiangliu had nine heads, a human face, a serpent's body, and was green in color. They dared not shoot northward, fearing the terrace of Gonggong. ���️ The terrace lies to its east. The terrace is square-shaped, with a snake at each corner; these snakes are tiger-colored, and their heads face southward.
7  海外北经:
深目国在其东，为人举一手一目，在共工台东。
The state of Shenmuguo lies to its east, where the people raise one hand with one eye; it is located east of Gonggong's terrace.
8  海外北经:
无肠之国在深目东，其为人长而无肠。
The state of Wuchangguo lies to the east of Shenmuguo, where its people are tall but have no intestines.
9  海外北经:
聂耳之国在无肠国东，使两文虎，为人两手聂其耳。县居海水中，及水所出入奇物。两虎在其东。
The state of Nieer Guo lies to the east of Wuchangguo, where two Wenhu are employed; its people use their two hands to hold their ears. It is suspended in the sea water, as well as at places where water enters and exits with strange objects. Two tigers lie to its east.
10  海外北经:
夸父与日逐走，入日。渴，欲得饮，饮于河渭；河渭不足，北饮大泽。未至，道渴而死。弃其杖，化为邓林。
Kuafu raced with the sun, chasing it until he reached the sun. He became thirsty and wanted to drink, so he drank from the rivers He and Wei; But the waters of He and Wei were insufficient, so he went northward to drink from a great marsh. Before reaching it, he died on the way from thirst. He discarded his staff, which transformed into Denglin.
11  海外北经:
博父国在聂耳东，其为人大，右手操青蛇，左手操黄蛇。邓林在其东，二树木。一曰博父。
The state of Bofu Guo lies to the east of Nieer, where its people are large in size; they hold a green snake in their right hand and a yellow snake in their left. Denglin lies to its east with two kinds of trees. One account says it is Bofu.
12  海外北经:
禹所积石之山在其东，河水所入。
The mountain where Yu piled up stones lies to its east, the place where the Yellow River flows into it.
13  海外北经:
拘缨之国在其东，一手把缨。一曰利缨之国。
To the east of this is the country of Juying, whose people have one hand holding a ribbon. One account says it was the country of Liying.
14  海外北经:
寻木长千里，在拘缨南，生河上西北。
The tree Xunmu, which is a thousand li tall, lies to the south of Juying; it grows on the northwest bank of the river.
15  海外北经:
跂踵国在拘缨东，其为人大，两足亦大，一曰大踵。
The country of Qizhong lies to the east of Juying; its people are tall, and their two feet are also large. One account says they were called Dazhong.
16  海外北经:
欧丝之野在大踵东，一女子跪据树欧丝。
The wilds of Ousi lie to the east of Dazhong, where a single woman is kneeling by a tree, spitting out silk.
17  海外北经:
桑无枝，在欧丝东，其木长百仞，无枝。
Sangwuzhi lies to the east of Ousi; its trees grow a hundred ren tall and have no branches.
18  海外北经:
范林方三百里，在三桑东，洲环其下。
Fanlin, square in shape and three hundred li in size, lies to the east of Sansang; islands surround its base.
19  海外北经:
务隅之山，帝颛顼葬于阳，九嫔葬于阴。一曰爰有熊、罴、文虎、离朱、𩿨久、视肉。
Mount Wuyu: Emperor Zhuanxu was buried on its southern side, and nine of his consorts were buried on the northern side. One account says there are also bears, brown bears, Wenhu tigers, Lizhu, Jiu, and Shirou here.
20  海外北经:
平丘在三桑东，爰有遗玉、青鸟、视肉、杨柳、甘柤、甘华，百果所生，在两山夹上谷，二大丘居中，名曰平丘。
Pingqiu lies to the east of Sansang. There, one may find Yuyu, green birds, Shirou, willows, Ganlou, and Ganhua; it is a place where all kinds of fruits grow. It is located in an upper valley between two mountains, with two large mounds at its center, named Pingqiu.
21  海外北经:
北海内有兽，其状如马，名曰騊駼。有兽焉，其名曰驳，状如白马，锯牙，食虎豹。有素兽焉，状如马，名曰蛩蛩。有青兽焉，状如虎，名曰罗罗。
Inside the North Sea there is a beast resembling a horse; it is called Tuotu. There is also an animal there, named Bo, which resembles a white horse with saw-like teeth and feeds on tigers and leopards. ���️ There is also a white beast there, resembling a horse; its name is Qiongqiong. There is also a green beast there, resembling a tiger; its name is Luolu.
22  海外北经:
北方禺强，人面鸟身，珥两青蛇，践两青蛇。
In the north is Yuqiang, who has a human face and bird's body, with two green snakes adorning his ears and treading upon two green snakes.
