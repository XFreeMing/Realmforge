
1  海内经:
东海之内，北海之隅，有国名曰朝鲜、天毒，其人水居，偎人爱人。
Within the East Sea and at the corner of the North Sea, there is a country named Chaoxian and Tiandu; its people live by water, they are affectionate and love others.
2  海内经:
西海之内，流沙之中，有国名曰壑市。
Within the West Sea, among the flowing sands, there is a country named Heshi.
3  海内经:
西海之内，流沙之西，有国名曰沮叶。
Within the West Sea, to the west of the flowing sands, there is a county named Juye.
4  海内经:
流沙之西，有鸟山者，三水出焉。爰有黄金、璇瑰、丹货、银铁，皆流于此中。又有淮山，好水出焉。
To the west of the Flowing Sands, there is a Bird Mountain from which three rivers originate. There are gold, xuan gui, red goods, silver and iron, all flowing in this area. There is also a Huai Mountain from which the Hao River originates.
5  海内经:
流沙之东，黑水之西，有朝云之国、司彘之国。黄帝妻雷祖，生昌意，昌意降处若水，生韩流。韩流擢首、谨耳、人面、豕喙、麟身、渠股、豚止，取淖子曰阿女，生帝颛顼。
To the east of the Flowing Sands and to the west of the Black Water, there are the countries of Chaoyun and Sizhi. Huangdi married Lei Zu, and they had Changyi; Changyi descended to live by the Ruoshui River, where he gave birth to Hanliu. Hanliu had an upturned head, cautious ears, a human face, a pig's mouth, a qilin body, channel-like thighs, and a pig-like tail. He took Naozi named Anv, and they gave birth to Emperor Zhuanxu.
6  海内经:
流沙之东，黑水之间，有山名不死之山。
To the east of Flowing Sands, between the Black Water, there is a mountain named "Bu Si Zhi Shan," the Mountain of Immortality.
7  海内经:
华山青水之东，有山名曰肇山，有人名曰柏高，柏高上下于此，至于天。
To the east of Mount Huashan and Qing Shui, there is a mountain called Zhaoshan. There is a person named Baigao; Baigao ascended and descended here to reach Heaven.
8  海内经:
西南黑水之间，有都广之野，后稷葬焉。爰有膏菽、膏稻、膏黍、膏稷，百谷自生，冬夏播琴。鸾鸟自歌，凤鸟自儛，灵寿实华，草木所聚。爰有百兽，相群爰处。此草也，冬夏不死。
Between the southwest and Black Water, there is an expanse called Duguang Ye, where Hou Ji was buried. There are bean oil, rice oil, millet oil, and grain oil; a hundred grains grow by themselves, sown in winter and summer. The Luan bird sings by itself, the Feng bird dances by itself; the Ling Shou tree bears flowers and fruits, where grasses and trees gather. There are a hundred kinds of beasts living together in groups here. This grass does not die in winter or summer.
9  海内经:
南海之内，黑水、青水之间，有木名曰若木，若水出焉。
Within the South Sea, between the Black Water and Qing Shui rivers, there is a tree named Ruomu; the Ruo River originates from it.
10  海内经:
有禺中之国。有列襄之国。有灵山，有赤蛇在木上，名曰蝡蛇，木食。
There is a country called Yuzhong. There is a country named Liexiang. There is a Ling Mountain, and on the trees there is a red snake called Rusan, which feeds on wood.
11  海内经:
有盐长之国。有人焉鸟首，名曰鸟氏。
There is a country named Yanchang. There are people with bird heads, named Niushi.
12  海内经:
有九丘，以水络之：名曰陶唐之丘、有叔得之丘、孟盈之丘、昆吾之丘、黑白之丘、赤望之丘、参卫之丘、武夫之丘、神民之丘。有木，青叶紫茎，玄华黄实，名曰建木，百仞无枝，有九欘，下有九枸，其实如麻，其叶如芒，大皞爰过，黄帝所为。
There are nine hills, connected by water: named Taotang Qiu, Shude Qiu, Mengying Qiu, Kunwu Qiu, Heibai Qiu, Chiwang Qiu, Canwei Qiu, Wufu Qiu, and Shenmin Qiu. There is a tree with green leaves and purple stems, black flowers and yellow fruits; it is called Jianmu. It is hundreds of ren high without branches, having nine supports above and nine gous below. Its fruit resembles hemp, its leaves resemble awns. Dayao passed by here, and this was made by Huangdi.
13  海内经:
有窫窳，龙首，是食人。有青兽，人面，名曰猩猩。
There is a Yaya, with a dragon's head; it eats people. There is a green beast with a human face, named Xingxing.
14  海内经:
西南有巴国。大皞生咸鸟，咸鸟生乘厘，乘厘生后照，后照是始为巴人。
To the southwest there is the country of Ba. Dayao gave birth to Xianniao, who in turn gave birth to Chengli; Chengli gave birth to Houzhao. It was Houzhao who first became the Ba people.
15  海内经:
有国名曰流黄辛氏，其域中方三百里，其出是尘土。有巴遂山，渑水出焉。
There is a country named Liu Huang Xin Shi, its territory square and three hundred li in size; what comes out of it is dust. There is a Basui Mountain from which the Mian River originates.
16  海内经:
又有朱卷之国。有黑蛇，青首，食象。
There is also a country named Zhuanjuan. There is a black snake with a green head that eats elephants.
17  海内经:
南方有赣巨人，人面长臂，黑身有毛，反踵，见人笑亦笑，唇蔽其面，因即逃也。
To the south there are giant people of Gan, with human faces and long arms, black bodies covered in hair, backward heels. When they see a person smile, they also smile; their lips cover their faces, so they immediately escape.
18  海内经:
又有黑人，虎首鸟足，两手持蛇，方啗之。
There are also black people with tiger heads and bird feet, holding snakes in both hands, just about to eat them.
19  海内经:
有羸民，鸟足。有封豕。
There is a weak people with bird feet. There are fengshi (giant boars).
20  海内经:
有人曰苗民。有神焉，人首蛇身，长如辕，左右有首，衣紫衣，冠旃冠，名曰延维，人主得而飨食之，伯天下。
There is a people called Miaomin. There is a deity, with a human head and snake body, as long as a 辕 (a part of an ancient chariot). On both sides are heads; it wears purple robes and a zhan crown. Its name is Yanwei. A ruler who captures and sacrifices to it will become the hegemon of the world.
21  海内经:
有鸾鸟自歌，凤鸟自儛。凤鸟首文曰德，翼文曰顺，膺文曰仁，背文曰义，见则天下和。
There are Luan birds singing by themselves, Feng birds dancing by themselves. The Feng bird has a head pattern named "De" (Virtue), wing patterns named "Shun" (Obedience), breast patterns named "Ren" (Benevolence), and back patterns named "Yi" (Righteousness). When it appears, the world will be in harmony.
22  海内经:
又有青兽如菟，名曰𡹤狗。有翠鸟。有孔鸟。
There is also a green beast like a rabbit, named Tugou. There are qiu birds (kingfishers). There is the Kong bird.
23  海内经:
南海之内有衡山。有菌山。有桂山。有山名三天子之都。
Within the South Sea there is a Heng Mountain. There is a Jun Mountain. There is a Gui Mountain. There is a mountain named Sandianshi Zhi Du, the Capital of Three Emperors.
24  海内经:
南方苍梧之丘，苍梧之渊，其中有九嶷山，舜之所葬，在长沙零陵界中。
To the south is Cangwu Qiu, and in the depths of Cangwu Yuan. Among them lies Jiuyi Mountain, where Emperor Shun was buried; it lies within the borders of Changsha Lingling.
25  海内经:
北海之内，有蛇山者，蛇水出焉，东入于海。有五彩之鸟，飞蔽一乡，名曰翳鸟。又有不距之山，巧倕葬其西。
Within the North Sea there is a She Mountain, from which the She River originates and flows eastward into the sea. There are five-colored birds that fly over an entire village, named Yiniu. There is also a Buju Mountain; Qiaochui was buried to its west.
26  海内经:
北海之内，有反缚盗械、带戈常倍之佐，名曰相顾之尸。
Within the North Sea, there are thieves bound in reverse fetters and carrying weapons, always doubling as assistants; they are named Xianggu Shishi.
27  海内经:
伯夷父生西岳，西岳生先龙，先龙是始生氐羌，氐羌乞姓。
Boyifu gave birth to Xiyue, who in turn gave birth to Xianlong; it was Xianlong who first gave rise to the Di and Qiang peoples, from whom the Di Qiang took their surname.
28  海内经:
北海之内，有山，名曰幽都之山，黑水出焉。其上有玄鸟、玄蛇、玄豹、玄虎、玄狐蓬尾。有大玄之山。有玄丘之民。有大幽之国。有赤胫之民。
Within the North Sea there is a mountain named Youdu Mountain; the Black River originates from it. There are black birds, black snakes, black leopards, black tigers, and black foxes with fluffy tails there. There is a Da Xuan Mountain. There are people of Xuanqiu. There is a country named Dayou. There are people with red shins.
29  海内经:
有钉灵之国，其民从膝已下有毛，马蹄善走。
There is a country named Dingling; its people have hair from the knees downward and horse-like hooves, making them good at running.
30  海内经:
炎帝之孙伯陵，伯陵同吴权之妻阿女缘妇，缘妇孕三年，是生鼓、延、殳。始为侯，鼓、延是始为锺，为乐风。
Yandi's grandson Boling had an affair with the wife of Wuquan, Anv Yuanfu. Yuanfu became pregnant for three years and gave birth to Gu, Yan, and Shu. It was then that Hou began; it was Gu and Yan who first made bells, creating musical styles.
31  海内经:
黄帝生骆明，骆明生白马，白马是为鲧。
Huangdi gave birth to Luoming, who in turn gave birth to Baoma; Baoma was known as Gun.
32  海内经:
帝俊生禺号，禺号生淫梁，淫梁生番禺，是始为舟。番禺生奚仲，奚仲生吉光，吉光是始以木为车。
Emperor Jun gave birth to Yuhao, who in turn gave birth to Yinliang; Yinliang gave birth to Fanyu, who first made boats. Fanyu gave birth to Xizhong, who in turn gave birth to Jiguang; it was Jiguang who first made carts from wood.
33  海内经:
少皞生般，般是始为弓矢。
Shaohao gave birth to Ban, who first began making bows and arrows.
34  海内经:
帝俊赐羿彤弓素矰，以扶下国，羿是始去恤下地之百艰。
Emperor Jun bestowed upon Yi a red bow and white arrow, to assist the lower states; it was Yi who first began to care for the many hardships of the people below.
35  海内经:
帝俊生晏龙，晏龙是为琴瑟。
Emperor Jun gave birth to Yanlong, and Yanlong became the qin and se (stringed instruments).
36  海内经:
帝俊有子八人，是始为歌儛。
Emperor Jun had eight sons; it was they who first began singing and dancing.
37  海内经:
帝俊生三身，三身生义均，义均是始为巧倕，是始作下民百巧。后稷是播百谷。稷之孙曰叔均，是始作牛耕。大比赤阴，是始为国。禹鲧是始布土，均定九州。
Emperor Jun gave birth to Sanshen, who in turn gave birth to Yijun; it was Yijun who first became Qiaochui and began making the many skills of the common people. Hou Ji sowed a hundred grains. The grandson of Ji was named Shujun, who first began plowing with oxen. Dabi Chiyin; it was he who first established a country. Yu and Gun were the ones who first distributed the land, evenly settling the Nine Provinces.
38  海内经:
炎帝之妻，赤水之子听訞生炎居，炎居生节并，节并生戏器，戏器生祝融，祝融降处于江水，生共工，共工生术器，术器首方颠，是复土穰，以处江水。共工生后土，后土生噎鸣，噎鸣生岁十有二。
Yandi's wife, the son of Chishui named Tingyue, gave birth to Yanju; Yanju gave birth to Jiebing, who in turn gave birth to Xiqi. Xiqi gave birth to Zhurong, who descended and lived by the Jiang River, giving birth to Gonggong. Gonggong gave birth to Shuqi, whose head was square on top; it was he who restored fertile soil and settled along the Jiang River. Gonggong gave birth to Houtu, who in turn gave birth to Yaming; Yaming gave birth to twelve sons representing the years.
39  海内经:
洪水滔天。鲧窃帝之息壤以堙洪水，不待帝命。帝令祝融杀鲧于羽郊。鲧复生禹。帝乃命禹卒布土以定九州。
Floodwaters surged up to the sky. Gun stole Emperor's Xi Rang (a divine soil) to block the floodwaters, without waiting for the emperor's command. The emperor ordered Zhurong to kill Gun at Yujiao. Gun gave birth once more to Yu. The emperor then ordered Yu to finally distribute the land and settle the Nine Provinces.
