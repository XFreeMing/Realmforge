
1 打开字典	海内北经:	
海内西北陬以东者。
All regions to the east of the northwest corner of the realm.
2 打开字典	海内北经:	
蛇巫之山，上有人操杯而东向立。一曰龟山。
The Shewu Mountains have a person on top holding a cup and standing facing east. It is also called Guishan.
3 打开字典显示相似段落	海内北经:	
西王母梯几而戴胜杖，其南有三青鸟，为西王母取食。在昆仑虚北。
Xi Wangmu leans on a step and holds a staff decorated with a headdress; to the south of her are three green birds that fetch food for Xi Wangmu. It is located north of Kunlun Xū.
4 打开字典显示相似段落	海内北经:	
有人曰大行伯，把戈。其东有犬封国，贰负之尸在大行伯东。
There is a person named Daxing Bo holding a halberd. To the east of him is the Dog Sealing State, and the corpse of Erfu lies to the east of Daxing Bo.
5 打开字典显示相似段落	海内北经:	
犬封国曰犬戎国，状如犬。有一女子，方跪进柸食。有文马，缟身朱鬣，目若黄金，名曰吉量，乘之寿千岁。
The Dog Sealing State is also called the Dog Rong State; its people look like dogs. There is a woman kneeling and presenting a cup of food. There are patterned horses with white bodies, red manes, golden eyes; they are named Jiliang. Riding one grants a lifespan of a thousand years.
6 打开字典显示相似段落	海内北经:	
鬼国在贰负之尸北，为物人面而一目，一曰贰负神在其东，为物人面蛇身。
The Ghost State lies to the north of Erfu's corpse. Its people have human faces and one eye. Another account says that the Erfu deity is located to the east, with a human face and a snake body.
7 打开字典显示相似段落	海内北经:	
蜪犬如犬，青，食人从首始。
The Lianquan looks like a dog, is blue in color, and eats people starting from their heads.
8 打开字典显示相似段落	海内北经:	
穷奇状如虎，有翼，食人从首始，所食被发，在蜪犬北。一曰从足。
lington Qiongqi resembles a tiger with wings; it eats people beginning from the head, and those it devours have long hair. It is located to the north of Lianquan. One account says it starts from the feet.
9 打开字典显示相似段落	海内北经:	
帝尧台、帝喾台、帝丹朱台、帝舜台，各二台，台四方，在昆仑东北。
The terrace of Emperor Yao, the terrace of Emperor Ku, the terrace of Emperor Dan Zhu, and the terrace of Emperor Shun—each has two terraces. The terraces are square in shape and located to the northeast of Kunlun.
10 打开字典	海内北经:	
大𧔧其状如螽。朱蛾其状如蛾。
Dafu looks like a katydid. Zhu'e resembles a moth in appearance.
11 打开字典	海内北经:	
蟜，其为人虎文，胫有𦜹。在穷奇东。一曰，状如人。昆仑虚北所有。
Jiao has tiger-like patterns on its body and bristles on its shins as a human. It is located to the east of Qiongqi. One account says it looks like a human. It is found in the north of Kunlun Xu.
12 打开字典	海内北经:	
闒非，人面而兽身，青色。
Tufei has a human face and an animal body, with a green color.
13 打开字典	海内北经:	
据比之尸，其为人折颈被发，无一手。
The corpse of Jubu has a broken neck, long hair, and is missing one hand as a human being.
14 打开字典	海内北经:	
环拘，其为人兽首人身，一曰猬状如狗，黄色。
Huanju has an animal head and a human body; another account says Wei resembles a dog, with a yellow color.
15 打开字典	海内北经:	
𥘯，其为物人身黑首从目。
Yan is a creature with a black head, human body, and eyes aligned in sequence.
16 打开字典	海内北经:	
戎，其为人人首三角。
Rong has three horns on its human face as a person.
17 打开字典显示相似段落	海内北经:	
林氏国有珍兽，大若虎，五彩毕具，尾长于身，名曰驺吾，乘之日行千里。
The Linshi State has rare beasts, large like tigers, with all five colors present; their tails are longer than their bodies. They are named Zouwu. Riding one allows a journey of a thousand li in a day.
18 打开字典显示相似段落	海内北经:	
昆仑虚南所，有泛林方三百里。
To the south of Kunlun Xu is Fanlin, an area measuring three hundred li on each side.
19 打开字典显示相似段落	海内北经:	
从极之渊深三百仞，维冰夷恒都焉，冰夷人面，乘两龙。一曰忠极之渊。
The Congji Spring is 300 ren deep; Bingyi people always reside there. Bingyi have human faces and ride two dragons. One account says it is called the Zhongji Spring.
20 打开字典显示相似段落	海内北经:	
阳污之山，河出其中；凌门之山，河出其中。
The Yangwu Mountains, from which the Yellow River originates; The Lingmen Mountains, from which the river flows out.
21 打开字典	海内北经:	
王子夜之尸，两手、两股、胸、首、齿，皆断异处。
The corpse of Wangzi Ye has both hands, both thighs, chest, head, and teeth all severed and lying in different places.
22 打开字典	海内北经:	
舜妻登比氏生宵明、烛光，处河大泽，二女之灵能照此所方百里。一曰登北氏。
Shun's wife Dengbi Shi gave birth to Xiaoming and Zhuguang, who lived in the great marsh of the Yellow River. The spirits of these two daughters could illuminate a radius of one hundred li around this place. One account says her name was Dengbei Shi.
23 打开字典	海内北经:

The Gai State lies to the south of Ju Yan and to the north of Wa. Wa belongs to Yan.
24 打开字典	海内北经:

Chaoxian lies to the east of Lieyang, north of the sea and south of the mountains. Lieyang belongs to Yan.

Liegu She lies in the middle of an island between the sea and the Yellow River.

The Gu She State is located in the sea, belonging to Liegu She; it lies to the southwest, surrounded by mountains.
