The Shishui River originates here, then flows north into Gaoze; it contains many Liyu fish and many patterned shells. There is an animal here: It resembles a pig but has teeth; it is called Dangkang. Its call announces its presence, and when seen, there will be an abundant harvest in the world.

There are many Ku Yu fish in it, which resemble ordinary fish but have bird wings. When they enter or leave the water, there is a glow; their sound resembles that of mandarin ducks. It is called Heyu, whose sound resembles that of an infant. This animal eats humans as well as insects and snakes; when it appears, there will be great floods throughout the world.

Two hundred li east lies Taishan, whose upper parts are rich in gold and jade as well as zhen wood. There is an animal here, resembling a cow with a white head, one eye and a snake's tail. It is called Fei; when it walks over water, the water dries up, and when it treads on grass, the grass dies. When it appears, there will be a major epidemic throughout the world. The Goushui River originates here and flows north into Laoshui; it contains many Liyu Fish.
