
1 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
海外自西南陬至西北陬者。
The regions overseas from the southwest corner to the northwest corner.
2 打开字典	海外西经:	
灭蒙鸟在结匈国北，为鸟青，赤尾。
Mie Mengniao is located north of Jiehung Guo; it is a bird with blue feathers and a red tail.
3 打开字典	海外西经:	
大运山高三百仞，在灭蒙鸟北。
Dayun Mountain, three hundred ren high, lies to the north of Mie Mengniao.
4 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
大乐之野，夏后启于此儛九代；乘两龙，云盖三层。左手操翳，右手操环，佩玉璜。在大运山北。一曰大遗之野。
In the plains of Dale, Xia Hou Qi danced here the Nine Generations; He rode two dragons, with three layers of clouds covering above. His left hand held a parasol, his right hand held a ring, and he wore a jade huang pendant. It is located to the north of Dayun Mountain. One name for it is Dayi's plains.
5 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
身国在夏后启北，一首而三身。
Shen Guo lies to the north of Xia Hou Qi, with one head and three bodies.
6 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
臂国在其北，一臂一目一鼻孔。有黄马虎文，一目而一手。
Biguo is located to its north; it has one arm, one eye, and one nostril. There are yellow horses with tiger-like patterns, each having one eye and one hand.
7 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
奇肱之国在其北，其人一臂三目，有阴有阳，乘文马。有鸟焉，两头，赤黄色，在其旁。
The country of Qigong lies to its north; the people there have one arm and three eyes, with both yin and yang characteristics, riding patterned horses. There is a bird there with two heads and reddish-yellow coloration, found nearby.
8 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
形天与帝至此争神，帝断其首，葬之常羊之山，乃以乳为目，以脐为口，操干戚以舞。
Xingtian and the Emperor came here to contend for supremacy; the Emperor severed his head, burying it at Changyang Mountain. Thus, he used his nipples as eyes and his navel as a mouth, holding a shield and axe to dance.
9 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
女祭、女戚在其北，居两水间，戚操鱼䱉，祭操俎。
Nujì and Nüqi are located to its north, living between two rivers; Qi holds a fish spear, while Ji holds an altar table.
10 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
䳐鸟、𪆻鸟，其色青黄，所经国亡。在女祭北。䳐鸟人面，居山上。一曰维鸟、青鸟、黄鸟所集。
The bird Shu and the bird Hui have blue-yellow colors; any country they pass through will perish. It is located to the north of Nujì. The bird Shu has a human face and dwells on mountains. One name for it is the gathering place of Weiniao, Qingniao, and Huangniao birds.
11 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
丈夫国在维鸟北，其为人衣冠带剑。
Zhuanfu Guo lies to the north of Weiniǎo; its people wear clothes, hats, sashes, and carry swords.
12 打开字典	海外西经:	
女丑之尸，生而十日炙杀之。在丈夫北，以右手鄣其面，十日居上，女丑居山之上。
The corpse of Nouchou was born but died ten days later from being scorched to death by the sun's heat. It is located north of Zhuanfu; covering its face with the right hand, it resides above for ten days, while Nouchou dwells on top of a mountain.
13 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
巫咸国在女丑北，右手操青蛇，左手操赤蛇，在登葆山，群巫所从上下也。
Wuxian Guo lies to the north of Nouchou; its people hold a green snake in their right hands and a red snake in their left, located at Dengbao Mountain, where groups of shamans ascend and descend.
14 打开字典	海外西经:	
并封在巫咸东，其状如彘，前后皆有首，黑。
Bingfeng lies to the east of Wuxian; its appearance resembles a pig, with heads both in front and behind, black in color.
15 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
女子国在巫咸北，两女子居，水周之。一曰居一门中。
Nüzi Guo lies to the north of Wuxian; two women live there, surrounded by water. One name for it is "residing in a single gate."
16 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
轩辕之国在此穷山之际，其不寿者八百岁。在女子国北，人面蛇身，尾交首上。
Xuanyuan Guo lies at the edge of this Qiong Mountain; those who do not live long there still live eight hundred years. It is located to the north of Nu Zhi Guo, with human faces and snake bodies, their tails coiled around their heads.
17 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
穷山在其北，不敢西射，畏轩辕之丘。在轩辕国北，其丘方，四蛇相绕。
Qiong Mountain lies to its north; one dares not shoot westward, fearing Xuanyuan Qiu. It is located to the north of Xuanyuan Guo; its hills are square-shaped, with four snakes coiled around them.
18 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
此诸夭之野，鸾鸟自歌，凤鸟自舞；凤皇卵，民食之；甘露，民饮之，所欲自从也。百兽相与群居。在四蛇北，其人两手操卵食之，两鸟居前导之。
In this expanse of these young ones' plains, the luan birds sing by themselves, and the phoenixes dance by themselves; phoenix eggs are consumed by the people; sweet dew is drunk by them, and whatever they desire follows of its own accord. All kinds of beasts live together in groups. It is located to the north of the four snakes; its people hold eggs with both hands and eat them, while two birds dwell in front guiding them.
19 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
龙鱼陵居在其北，状如狸。一曰鰕。即有神圣乘此以行九野。一曰鳖鱼在夭野北，其为鱼也如鲤。
Longyu Lingju lies to its north, shaped like a weasel. One name for it is Xia. Thus, there are divine and holy beings who ride this to travel the Nine Wilds. One name for it is Bieyu, located to the north of Yaoye; as a fish, it resembles a carp.
20 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
白民之国在龙鱼北，白身被发。有乘黄，其状如狐，其背上有角，乘之寿二千岁。
Baimin Guo lies to the north of Longyu; its people have white bodies and are covered in hair. There is a Shenghuang, which resembles a fox in appearance, with horns on its back; riding it grants a lifespan of two thousand years.
21 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
肃慎之国在白民北，有树名曰雄常，先入代帝，于此取之。
Sushen Guo lies to the north của Baimin; there is a tree named Xiongchang, which was taken here by those who first entered and replaced the Emperor.
22 打开字典显示相似段落	海外西经:	
长股之国在雄常北，被发。一曰长脚。
Changgu Guo lies to the north of Xiongchang; its people are covered in hair. One name for it is Changjiao.
23 打开字典	海外西经:	
西方蓐收，左耳有蛇，乘两龙。
In the west, Rushou has a snake in his left ear and rides two dragons.
