
1 打开字典	海内南经:	
海内东南陬以西者。
The western regions from the southeasternmost part of the realm.
2 打开字典	海内南经:	
瓯居海中。闽在海中，其西北有山。一曰闽中山在海中。
Ou is located in the sea. Min lies in the sea, and there are mountains to its northwest. One account says that Zhongshan of Min is located in the sea.
3 打开字典	海内南经:	
天子鄣山在闽西海北。一曰在海中。
Emperor's Zhang Mountain lies to the north of the western sea of Min. One account says it is located in the sea.
4 打开字典显示相似段落	海内南经:	
桂林八树在番隅东。
The Eight Trees of Guilin are located to the east of Fanyu.

伯虑国、离耳国、雕题国、北朐国皆在郁水南。郁水出湘陵、南山。一曰相虑。
lou The states of Bolv, Le'er, Diaoti, and Beiwu are all located to the south of Yushui. Yushui originates from Xiangling and Nan Shan. One account says Xianglv.
6 打开字典显示相似段落	海内南经:	
枭阳国在北朐之西，其为人人面长唇，黑身有毛，反踵，见人笑亦笑，左手操管。
The state of Xiaoyang lies to the west of Beiwu. The people there have human faces and long lips, black bodies covered with hair, reversed heels, and when they see someone laughing, they also laugh; they hold a tube in their left hand.
7 打开字典	海内南经:	
兕在舜葬东，湘水南，其状如牛，苍黑，一角。
The si is located east of the tomb of Shun and south of Xiangshui; it resembles a cow, has a blue-black color, and one horn.
8 打开字典显示相似段落	海内南经:	
苍梧之山，帝舜葬于阳，帝丹朱葬于阴。
Cangwu Mountain: Emperor Shun was buried on its southern side, and Emperor Danzhu was buried on its northern side.
9 打开字典显示相似段落	海内南经:	
泛林方三百里，在狌狌东。
Fanlin spans three hundred li in area, located to the east of Shengsheng.
10 打开字典显示相似段落	海内南经:	
狌狌知人名，其为兽如豕而人面，在舜葬西。
Shengsheng knows human names; as an animal it resembles a pig with a human face, and is located to the west of Shun's tomb.
11 打开字典	海内南经:	
狌狌西北有犀牛，其状如牛而黑。
To the northwest of Shengsheng there are rhinoceroses; they resemble cows but are black in color.
12 打开字典显示相似段落	海内南经:	
夏后启之臣曰孟涂，是司神于巴，人请讼于孟涂之所，其衣有血者乃执之，是请生。居山上，在丹山西。丹山在丹阳南，丹阳居属也。
A minister of Xia Hou Qi was named Mengtu, who presided over spirits in Ba. When people brought lawsuits to the place of Mengtu, those whose clothes were stained with blood would be arrested; this is called requesting life. Jushan Mountain lies west of Dan Shan. Dan Shan is located south of Danyang, which belongs to the jurisdiction of Juyu.
13 打开字典显示相似段落	海内南经:	
窫窳龙首，居弱水中，在狌狌知人名之西，其状如龙首，食人。
Yayu Longshou dwells in Ruoshui, west of the place where Shengsheng knows human names. It has a dragon-like head and eats people.
14 打开字典显示相似段落	海内南经:	
有木，其状如牛，引之有皮，若缨、黄蛇。其叶如罗，其实如栾，其木若蓲，其名曰建木，在窫窳西弱水上。
There is a tree; its appearance resembles that of an ox, with a hide that can be pulled out, like a tassel and yellow snake. Its leaves resemble those of the luo plant, its fruit resembles that of the luanchou tree, and its wood is like that of the shui plant; it is called Jianmu. It grows on Ruoshui to the west of Yayu.
15 打开字典显示相似段落	海内南经:	
氐人国在建木西，其为人人面而鱼身，无足。
The state of Diren lies to the west of Jianmu; its people have human faces and fish bodies, without feet.
16 打开字典显示相似段落	海内南经:	
巴蛇食象，三岁而出其骨，君子服之，无心腹之疾。其为蛇青黄赤黑。一曰黑蛇青首，在犀牛西。
lou The Ba snake eats an elephant, and after three years it spits out the bones; gentlemen who consume them will be free from internal ailments. Its color as a snake is green, yellow, red, and black. One account says it was a black snake with a green head, located west of the rhinoceros.
17 打开字典	海内南经:	
旄马，其状如马，四节有毛。在巴蛇西北，高山南。
The Mao Ma resembles a horse and has hair on its four joints. It lies northwest of the Ba snake, south of a high mountain.
18 打开字典	海内南经:	
匈奴、开题之国、列人之国并在西北。
The Xiongnu, Kai Ti state, and Lie Ren state are all located in the northwest.
